## 🤝 Call for Contributors (Add Your Company!)

We want to make this map as comprehensive and up-to-date as possible! If your company is missing, or if you know of a tech company, startup, marketplace, or IT hub in Almaty that should be on this list, please contribute!

**How to contribute:**
1. **Fork** this repository.
2. **Add** the missing company's details (Name, Cluster/Category, Latitude, Longitude) to our dataset.
3. **Submit a Pull Request** for review.

Alternatively, you can simply open an **Issue** with the company name, its category, and location coordinates, and we will add it for you.
